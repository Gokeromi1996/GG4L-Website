About Us

GG4L is a collective of dreamers, storytellers, and beatmakers who believe in the transformative power of music. Based in the heart of NYC, we strive to nurture local talent and bring their voices to a global stage, staying true to the roots of hip-hop while pushing its boundaries.

Stay tuned for upcoming releases, live shows, and exclusive content from your favorite borough legends!

Featured Artists

JayQuellz (Bronx)

Known for his electrifying punchlines and storytelling flair, JayQuellz channels the energy of the Bronx into every track. Raised amidst the birthplace of hip-hop, his music blends gritty realism with vibrant beats, paying homage to the borough's rich rap legacy.

Neon Nyah (Brooklyn)

A lyrical powerhouse with a voice as dynamic as the Brooklyn skyline, Neon Nyah brings unapologetic fire to the mic. Her tracks reflect the hustle, resilience, and creativity of a borough that never sleeps.

EastSide Eli (Manhattan)

EastSide Eli takes listeners on a journey through Manhattan's vibrant underground rap scene. With a smooth flow and thought-provoking lyrics, Eli's sound bridges the gap between uptown energy and downtown sophistication.

Lil Wavey (Queens)

Lil Wavey's tracks are an eclectic mix of modern trap beats and old-school Queens flavor. A rising star known for his melodic hooks, he's redefining what it means to represent the borough's diverse rap culture.

Sha'Starr (Staten Island)

Staten Island's finest, Sha'Starr, infuses his sharp rhymes with the raw grit of his borough. His unique style combines cinematic storytelling with hard-hitting beats, making him a voice to watch in the NYC rap game.
